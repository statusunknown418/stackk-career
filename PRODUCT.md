# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Spanish-speaking job seekers, initially in Peru, who need a recruiter-ready CV, practical interview preparation, or affordable career coaching. Core users include candidates returning to the market, building a CV from scratch, preparing for an interview, or targeting multiple roles.

## Product Purpose

Assendia turns an existing PDF or blank page into a structured, editable CV, provides actionable AI analysis, and connects candidates with confidential human coaching. Success means visitors understand the value quickly, trust the product with career data, and start creating or improving a CV.

## Brand Personality

Practical, confident, humane. Expert guidance without consultant jargon, opaque scoring, or imported Silicon Valley tone. Spanish-first copy should feel direct, supportive, and locally relevant.

## Anti-references

Generic AI SaaS pages, purple glow dashboards, equal feature-card grids, vague transformation claims, score-only resume graders, inaccessible motion, and layouts that hide the primary action on mobile.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with a concrete career outcome and one clear next action.
2. Show editable work and real product value instead of abstract AI claims.
3. Earn trust through clarity, confidentiality, and transparent expectations.
4. Keep Spanish-first content concise, natural, and easy to scan.
5. Treat mobile as the primary acquisition surface, not a desktop fallback.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic HTML, visible keyboard focus, WCAG AA contrast, descriptive image alternatives, touch targets of at least 44px, and reduced-motion fallbacks. Preserve legibility at 320px width and browser zoom. Never rely on color or motion alone to communicate meaning.
